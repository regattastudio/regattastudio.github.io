Regatta Studio

Make scoring a breeze

Regatta Studio is a free cloud-based app for scoring informal one-design racing from your cellphone or tablet. Get rid of your pencils, clipboards, and complicated scoring programs. Scores are captured in realtime on the app and results are updated almost instantly. Competitors can create entries, view notices, and check results from their mobile devices. Regatta Studio is ideal for informal club racing and virtual sailing.

Everything in the cloud

All data is captured in the cloud in real time. Multiple users can enter data simultaneously and everyone sees the latest updates. Data is cached locally in case of network interruptions and will sync with the cloud when connectivity is restored.
